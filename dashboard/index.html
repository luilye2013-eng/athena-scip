  • Data Sources: This platform aggregates data from third-party RSS feeds (BBC, CNN, NPR, Al Jazeera), USGS (earthquakes), NOAA (weather alerts), and Open-Meteo (global weather). Commodity prices are sourced from Yahoo Finance.
  • Data Quality: Location data may be incomplete or inferred from event descriptions. Events classified as "Unknown" location or "Other" type have been flagged for review. Supply chain impact scores are algorithmic estimates and should be verified.
  • Limitations: The system automatically filters events that are not supply chain related. However, some relevant events may be missed, and some non-relevant events may be included.
